          Malnad College of Engineering established in the year 1960, during the second 5 year plan, as a joint venture of Government of India,
        Government of Karnataka and the Malnad Technical Education Society, Hassan.

        The Malnad College of Engineering is now a reputed Engineering college in the country.
        The college has earned “ISTE Award” as one of the Best Engineering Colleges in the Country, in the year 2007.
        The Institute located in the midway, (13°N and 76.5°E, altitude 943 mtrs.)
        between Bengaluru (180 kms) and Mangaluru (170 kms) on NH-75.
